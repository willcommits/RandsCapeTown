About Rands Cape Town

RANDS IS A RARE OUTDOOR LIFESTYLE SPACE

If you're the one for exclusivity or simply want to you can simply make reservations for a day of your choice. True to our heritage, we have the best local Acts / Bands on the line up for the best experience - from Thursday to Sunday we have a reputable line up to make sure the Rands experience is nothing short of Excellent.

Our culinary team prepares top Tshisa Nyama dishes in the land, with an open bar and affordable prices - we are the heaven of Entertainment when it comes to hosting the best crowd under the sun.
